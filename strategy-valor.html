Strategy · Value Loading project…

1. Target segment

The segment where this promise is aimed: who they are and in what situation.

2. Core problem / central tension

What is the main pain or tension from their point of view, not from the productʼs.

3. Value proposition (promise)

Simple, concrete and outcome-focused. Doesnʼt need to be pretty yet, just clear.

4. Key benefits

Functional, emotional and risk-related benefits that matter to this segment.

5. Differentiation

Why you vs. alternatives (including status quo and DIY solutions).

6. Proof points

Signals that make the promise believable: evidence, cases, metrics, learning loops…

7. Elevator pitch (v1)

First-pass story you’d use in a 30–60 second conversation.

8. Notes & narrative angles

Hooks, metaphors or angles that feel promising for later copy and storytelling.
